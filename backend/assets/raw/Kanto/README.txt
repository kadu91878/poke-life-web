Pokťmon Life!

This folder contains everything you need in order to play Pokťmon Life.
If you need help on how to assemble the board game, visit www.pokemonlife.net/buildingtheboardgame.php.
You may also ask questions on the forum at www.pokemonlife.net/forum

Much of the art used in making this game is not mine. If any art belongs to you, please contact me.

When printing the game, you may choose to not print the Victory Pile & Pokťmon.
Doing this will make the game less exciting, but it will save you work creating the game.
You can always print the Victory Pile & Pokťmon at a later time. 
When playing without the Victory Pile, everything that lets you draw a Victory card grants 10 Master Points instead.

I wish you a lot of luck in creating this board game, and I hope to hear from you on the forum!
